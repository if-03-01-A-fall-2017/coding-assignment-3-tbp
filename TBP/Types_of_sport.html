Team sports

Here are some Sporttypes which are described more properly.

Soccer

Soccer is a family of team sports that involves kicking a ball with the foot to score a goal.

In soccer, two teams of each 10 field players and 1 goalkeeper play against each other.

Team:A team is build of defenders, midfielders and attackers.
The different positions are mostly divided up so that every position is equally occupied.
Every team must have a goalkeeper, who is the only one allowed to play with their hands but only in the penalty area.
Rules also state that a football team must at least have 7 players.
Every team also consists of substitude players wich can be switched in during the game, how many are allowed in the team or how many are allowed to switch in is differed in all competitions.

Basketball

In Basketball, two teams of 5 players try to score points by shooting the ball through the basket of the opposing team.
